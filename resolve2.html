Proof

One we reshaped.

Aviation · Cross-border real estate & operations

A major European airline — overseas premises and operations reshaped.

Situation

Overseas operations spread across multiple countries, run on premises and processes that no longer fit the work. Set-ups were inconsistent, documentation fragmented, and continuity depended on the few people who knew how each location actually ran.

Intervention

Reshaped the footprint around how the work actually happens. Lease conditions were specially crafted to match operations across the entire portfolio — governed by one set of guiding rules, so every lease supported a pillar of the business rather than just a single site. Operations and facilities management were simplified into one repeatable model, for the teams in post locally and for the corporate real-estate and FM function.

Outcome

Costs reduced by around 20%, with the change implemented in weeks — seamless for the customer, as if the shift had happened in a single day, and smooth for the operations teams living it. Resilient premises, lease terms that hold, and an operation that no longer depends on who built it.
