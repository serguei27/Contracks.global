Proof

One we built to be backed.

Sustainable aviation fuel · Kenya / Luxembourg · Venture build

Bleriot — a sustainable aviation fuel venture, structured to raise.

Situation

A sustainable aviation fuel venture spanning East African feedstock and European offtake — a strong idea sitting on the hard part: the commercial structure, supply chain and certification path that make a venture genuinely investable.

Intervention

Built the venture's commercial and operational foundations — feedstock supply-chain strategy, airline offtake modelling, the certification pathway (ICAO/CORSIA, ASTM) and a fundraising architecture investors could underwrite. The 5Cs applied to a venture, not a project.

Outcome

What began as a sustainable aviation fuel concept became a structured venture platform spanning East African feedstock, European market access and a defined certification pathway. The venture moved from an idea requiring explanation to a business investors, strategic partners and offtakers could evaluate on commercial merit.
