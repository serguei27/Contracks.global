Proof

Aviation · Africa · 15-country portfolio

Brussels Airlines — portfolio framework, 15 countries.

Situation

A property and operations footprint spanning 15 African countries. Inconsistent documentation, fragmented condition reporting, and no single commercial framework holding the portfolio together.

Intervention

Designed a portfolio framework deployed across all 15 countries. Embedded LiDAR-based property assessment into the commercial reporting workflow. Brought the operations and commercial functions onto a shared view of the portfolio.

Outcome

Programme delivered approximately 10% below budget. The framework continued to operate after handover.
