Club 03 — The Iron · Full 90-Second Script

The 90-Second Mission Pitch

~90 seconds · Rating 9.8/10
8–10s
Hidden Theme
Projects don't fail. The systems around them do. Frontier markets just expose it faster.
35–45s
Personal Origin
I was 8. My dad cleared his desk to make room for boxes from Radio Shack — a computer you had to build yourself. We assembled it piece by piece until a black and white screen lit up. One evening Charlie came by — a friend of my parents, Afro-American, possibly Ghanaian origin, stationed at the US Army base nearby. He looked at what an 8-year-old had built and said: "There will be one in every office, car and plane. The easiest way to control systems." 1983. Most people had no idea what they were looking at.
10–12s
Business Origin
Equatorial Guinea. Forest. No supply chain. We threw out the imported model — community built the camp. One tenth the cost. Six months. No fence. State client released payment with one note: "We have started the works." Third bridge unlocked.
8–10s
Mission
My mission is to turn the person accountable for delivery into the person who actually delivers — in the markets where systems break first.
12–15s
3-Year Vision
In three years, 200+ operators across Europe, Africa, the UK and the US will be running our method on live projects — on time, on budget, without surprises. The Contracks certification will travel without us in the room.
8–10s
Big Vision
But ultimately — a world where the right projects get built. Not the ones that survive the best contract fight. The ones communities actually need. Charlie saw it in 1983. We're building it now.
