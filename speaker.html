Bio

I run Contracks Global and built the Delivery Control System™. I work with PMO Directors and commercial managers on cross-border builds from €100M to €700M — energy, infrastructure, aviation, real estate.

Twenty-five years across thirty countries taught me the same lesson over and over: projects don't fail. The systems around them do.

In Equatorial Guinea, the imported playbook said twelve months and a perimeter fence. We delivered in six. At a tenth of the cost. With no fence — the community built it. That project is where the method comes from.
